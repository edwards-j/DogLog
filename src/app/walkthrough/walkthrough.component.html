Welcome to DogLog! The goal of this application is to help those responsible for taking care of pets stay in sync. This way everyone knows when the animals have been fed, let out, recieved medicine, and more.

You'll be able to add your pets to your profile, create daily logs of whatever events you want to track, and share your pets with other users so everyone is up to date.

To create a pet, just click on the green + button from the home screen. This will open a form where you can enter the pet's name, species, and other information.

To add a daily log for a pet, select them from the home screen, then click on the green + button. This will add a new log for that day.

To add events for that day, click on the log, then click on the green + button next to the Events header. The events that you are tracking for that pet will show up. Just select the event you want to log, add a note if you'd like, then hit save.

The event will be added to the top of the list, and the daily summary at the top will be updated. To see more information about the event, such as who created it and any notes associated, click on the "..." icon on the right side of that event.

To share a pet with other users, select the pet from the home screen, then click on the gear icon in the top right. This is where you'll be able to edit pet info, share the pet with other users, and update the events you want to track.

Click the green share icon at the top and you'll be able to enter the email address of the user that you want to share this pet with. They'll have access to the pet and will be able to add new logs.

The pet's primary owner is the only one who can share a pet with other users, edit the pet's info, and change the events tracked.

And that's it, happy logging!
